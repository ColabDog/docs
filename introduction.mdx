## Welcome

MrAstro provides developers an easy way to tap into advanced AI Features with
just a couple lines of code.

There are a couple of concepts for MrAstro: 
- **collection** refers to a store of objects (similar to a table of rows in SQL)
- **object** is any JSON-like storage (similar to a row in SQL)
